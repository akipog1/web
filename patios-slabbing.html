About This Service

The Perfect Patio,
Perfectly Laid

A well-designed patio can completely transform how you use your outdoor space — turning an unused garden into a beautiful area for entertaining, relaxing and enjoying the Scottish outdoors.

At AB Paving, we've been installing patios and garden slabbing across Glasgow and Central Scotland for over 20 years. We work with a wide range of materials — from natural Indian sandstone and slate to modern porcelain slabs — and we'll help you choose what's right for your budget, style and lifestyle.

Every patio we lay is properly prepared from the ground up — correct sub-base, sharp sand bedding, proper pointing and drainage — so your patio looks great and lasts for years to come.

Materials & Options

Choose Your Perfect
Patio Material

🪨

Indian Sandstone

Classic, warm-toned and naturally textured. Indian sandstone is one of the most popular choices for Scottish gardens.

⬛

Natural Slate & Stone

Dark, contemporary and striking. Natural slate and grey stone patios create a modern look that complements any garden.

🔲

Porcelain Slabs

Low maintenance, highly durable and available in a huge range of finishes. Porcelain is an increasingly popular premium option.

📐

Full Ground Preparation

Proper excavation, compacted sub-base and sharp sand bedding ensure your patio won't sink or shift over time.

🌧️

Drainage & Falls

Correct gradients and drainage channels built in — so rainwater runs away rather than sitting on your patio.

✨

Pointing & Finishing

Professional pointing throughout, with clean sharp edges and a thorough tidy-up so your patio is ready to enjoy.
