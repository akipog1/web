✦ Before You Start — Do These 3 Things First

1. Set up your Formspree account (free — takes 5 minutes)

Your quote form needs this to actually send enquiries to your email. Without it, form submissions go nowhere.

How to do it 1. Go to formspree.io and create a free account

2. Once logged in, click + New Form

3. Give it a name like "AB Paving Quote Form"

4. Copy the endpoint URL — it looks like: https://formspree.io/f/abcd1234

5. Open index.html in Notepad (right-click → Open with → Notepad)

6. Press Ctrl+F and search for: YOUR_FORM_ID

7. Replace the whole action URL with your Formspree URL, e.g.:
action="https://formspree.io/f/abcd1234"

8. Save the file. Done — every form submission now emails you directly.
Use the same email you check daily. Formspree free plan gives you 50 submissions/month — more than enough to start.

2. Update the domain in your sitemap

Open sitemap.xml in Notepad. If your domain is NOT abpaving.co.uk, do a Find & Replace (Ctrl+H) and replace abpaving.co.uk with your actual domain. Save it.

3. Check your domain is pointed at Fasthosts

If you bought your domain elsewhere (e.g. GoDaddy, 123-reg), you'll need to point the nameservers at Fasthosts before the site will show up. Fasthosts support can help — call them on 0333 0142 700.

1 Upload Your Website to Fasthosts

Fasthosts gives you two ways to upload. Option A (File Manager) is easiest — no software to download.

Option A — Fasthosts File Manager (recommended)

Step by step 1. Log in to your Fasthosts control panel at control.fasthosts.co.uk

2. Click Web Hosting → select your hosting package

3. Click File Manager (in the Files section)

4. You'll see a folder called htdocs or public_html — this is your website root. Open it.

5. Delete any placeholder files already in there (like index.html or index.php)

6. Click Upload and upload all your website files. You need to upload:
  • All .html files (index.html, about.html, gallery.html etc.)
  • The css/ folder (with style.css, home.css, pages.css inside)
  • The js/ folder (with main.js inside)
  • The images/ folder (with all photos inside)
  • sitemap.xml

7. Wait for uploads to complete — images may take a couple of minutes
The index.html file must be directly inside htdocs/ — NOT inside a subfolder. If it ends up at htdocs/abpaving/index.html your site won't load.

Option B — FTP with FileZilla (more control)

  1. Download FileZilla free from filezilla-project.org
  2. In your Fasthosts control panel → FTP & SSH → note your FTP hostname, username and password
  3. Open FileZilla, enter the FTP details at the top, click Quickconnect
  4. On the right side of FileZilla, navigate into htdocs/
  5. On the left side, find your website files on your computer
  6. Select all files and folders and drag them across to the right panel
  7. Wait for all transfers to complete
FTP is better if you have a slow internet connection or are uploading lots of large photos, as it resumes if interrupted.

2 Turn On Free SSL (HTTPS)

SSL makes your site load as https:// instead of http://. Google requires this for good rankings and it shows a padlock to visitors — building trust. Fasthosts includes this free.

How to activate SSL on Fasthosts 1. In your Fasthosts control panel, go to Web Hosting → Manage

2. Look for SSL Certificates or Free SSL

3. Click Install or Activate — it runs automatically

4. Allow up to 24 hours for the certificate to issue (usually much faster)

5. Once active, your site loads at https://yourdomain.co.uk
If you're unsure, call Fasthosts on 0333 0142 700 and just say "I need to activate the free SSL certificate on my hosting." They'll walk you through it in 2 minutes.

3 Test Your Live Site

Once uploaded, visit your domain and go through this checklist. Tick everything off before you start promoting it.

  • Homepage loads correctly at your domain
  • Logo appears in the top-left corner
  • All photos load — hero image, service cards, gallery
  • Navigation menu works — click every link
  • Services dropdown appears on hover (desktop)
  • Hamburger menu opens and closes on mobile (test on your phone)
  • Phone number is clickable and dials on mobile
  • Floating "Free Quote – Call Now" button is visible
  • Quote form submits successfully and you receive an email
  • Gallery lightbox opens when you click a photo
  • Gallery filter buttons (All / Driveways / Patios / Landscaping) work
  • All footer links work on every page
  • Site loads as https:// (padlock visible in browser)

If photos aren't loading

Check that the images/ folder is directly inside htdocs/ and all image files are inside it. File names are case-sensitive on web servers — Hero-Driveway.jpg and hero-driveway.jpg are different files.

If the page looks unstyled (plain text)

The css/ folder hasn't uploaded correctly. Make sure style.css, home.css and pages.css are all inside a css/ folder within htdocs/.

4 Get on Google — This Brings in the Leads

Getting onto Google is far more important than any other marketing. Do these three things in week one.

4a. Google Business Profile — do this TODAY

This gets you into Google Maps and the local results panel. Most paving enquiries in Glasgow come from here, not from search results.

Set it up in 20 minutes 1. Go to business.google.com

2. Click Manage now → search for "AB Paving & Landscaping"

3. If it doesn't exist, click Add your business to Google

4. Business category: choose Paving Contractor (add Landscaper as secondary)

5. Add your full address, phone number, website URL and opening hours

6. Verify your listing — Google sends a postcard to your address with a code (takes 5–7 days)

7. Once verified, add photos: at least 10 project photos, your logo and a team photo
Ask every satisfied customer to leave a Google review. Even 10 good reviews will put you above most competitors in Glasgow search results.

4b. Google Search Console (free)

This tells Google your site exists and lets you monitor how people find you.

  1. Go to search.google.com/search-console
  2. Click Add Property → enter your domain
  3. Verify ownership — choose the HTML tag method and paste the code into the <head> section of index.html
  4. Once verified, click Sitemaps in the left menu
  5. Enter sitemap.xml and click Submit — this tells Google about all your pages

4c. Request Google to index your site

In Search Console, click URL Inspection, paste your homepage URL, and click Request Indexing. Repeat for each page. Google usually finds your site within 1–7 days.

5 What to Do in the First Month

Week 1 priority list:
✓ Site live and tested
✓ Formspree form connected and tested
✓ Google Business Profile created
✓ Share your new site on your Facebook page — "We've just launched our new website!"

Getting your first Google reviews (most important)

Call or text every recent customer and ask them to leave a Google review. Send them a direct link — find it in your Google Business Profile dashboard under "Get more reviews." Aim for 10 reviews in your first month. This alone will get you ranking above most Glasgow competitors.

Facebook posts that get enquiries

Post 2–3 times per week. The posts that work best are: before & after photos of completed jobs, photos taken during a job showing the team at work, and short videos of a finished driveway or patio. Always end with "Contact us for a free quote" and your phone number.

Consider Google Ads in month 2

Once you have a few reviews and your site is indexed, £5–10/day on Google Ads targeting "block paving Glasgow" and "driveway Glasgow" will get you leads almost immediately. It's the fastest way to fill your diary.

List on Checkatrade

Your site already mentions Checkatrade as a trust signal. If you're not yet listed, it's worth doing — homeowners in Glasgow specifically search for Checkatrade-approved tradespeople. A listing costs around £70–90/month but typically pays for itself with one job.

✦ Making Changes to Your Site Later

The site is built so that one file controls the look of every page. You only need to upload the file you changed — not everything.

  • To change colours, fonts, nav or footer → edit css/style.css
  • To change homepage layout → edit css/home.css
  • To change service/gallery/about pages → edit css/pages.css
  • To change wording or content → edit the relevant .html file
  • To swap a photo → upload a new image to images/ using the same filename
Always keep a backup copy of your site files on your computer. Before making any change, save the original file with a name like index-backup.html so you can restore it if anything goes wrong.
