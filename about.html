Our Story

Over 20 Years
Serving Glasgow

AB Paving & Landscaping has been transforming driveways, patios and gardens across Glasgow and Central Scotland for over two decades. We started as a small, local team with a simple belief: that every homeowner deserves quality work, honest pricing and a team they can actually trust.

That belief hasn't changed. Today we're still the same family-run business, still based locally, and still putting the same care and attention into every job — whether it's a small patio or a major garden transformation.

When you choose AB Paving, you're not just getting a driveway or a patio. You're getting a team that genuinely cares about getting it right.
